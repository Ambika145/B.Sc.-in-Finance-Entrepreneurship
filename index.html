Programme Overview

The B.Sc. in Finance & Entrepreneurship (Online) is a 3-year undergraduate programme by O.P. Jindal Global University, designed to build strong financial foundations and entrepreneurial skills for today's evolving business landscape.

The curriculum blends finance, business, and entrepreneurship with FinTech, data analytics, and artificial intelligence, helping learners understand how money, innovation, and technology work together in the real world.

Delivered in a 100% online format, the programme combines academic learning with practical exposure through case studies, simulations, masterclasses, and hands-on tools.

With a focus on real-world application and career readiness, it prepares learners for roles in finance, consulting, startups, or to build their own ventures.

Eligibility:

Academic Requirement

Minimum 50% in Class 12 from a recognised board.

Subject Requirement

Must have studied at least one of the following:

Mathematics / Business Mathematics / Statistics / Business Studies

Selection Criteria:

Admission is based on a multi-step evaluation process:

Step 1: Application Submission

Complete and submit the online application form.

Step 2: FACTA Assessment

Appear for the FACTA (Finance Aptitude and Critical Thinking Assessment) conducted by the university.

Step 3: Personal Interview

Shortlisted candidates will be invited for a personal interview.

Step 4: Final Selection

Admission is granted based on overall performance across the assessment and interview.
